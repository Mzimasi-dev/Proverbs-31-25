About Us

The Proverbs 31:25 Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting girls in Pietermaritzburg, South Africa, by providing them with essential sanitary products and empowering them to overcome challenges.

Through education, advocacy, and community support, the Foundation works to restore dignity, confidence, and opportunity to young girls facing period poverty and social inequality.

Our Vision & Mission Statement

Vision

To clothe girls with strength and dignity, empowering them to rise above challenges and shine for God's glory, as Proverbs 31:25 inspires.

Our vision is bold: a world where every girl walks confidently into her future, free from shame and limitation.

Mission

We provide essential resources, support, and mentorship to girls in need, reminding them of their worth and value in Christ, and enabling them to walk confidently into their future.
