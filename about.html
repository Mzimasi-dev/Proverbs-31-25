Proverbs 31:25 Foundation is dedicated to empowering women and girls through faith, education, health support, and advocacy. Your contribution helps us create opportunities, restore dignity, and build stronger communities.

Our Vision

To see empowered, confident women and girls who are equipped to walk boldly into their future with dignity and purpose.

Our Mission

To provide holistic support through education, mentorship, health initiatives, and faith-based programmes that transform lives.
